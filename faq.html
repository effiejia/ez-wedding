FAQs

Can I take photos during the ceremony? +

We kindly request an unplugged ceremony so everyone can be fully present in the moment. Cameras are very welcome during the weekend's other events.

Can I bring a plus one? +
If your invitation includes a plus one, we would be delighted to celebrate with them. Otherwise, we kindly ask that you attend solo. If you're unsure, please reach out to us directly.
Is there parking available? +
Yes, parking will be available at The Ashokan Center. However, we recommend using the shuttle service provided from Grand Central Station or carpooling, for convenience.
Is there transportation provided? +
We have arranged a shuttle to and from The Ashokan Center for the weekend. See the details here for more information. Please indicate in your RSVP whether you are planning to use the service so we can plan accordingly.
What should I do if I have dietary restrictions? +
Please mention any dietary requirements in your RSVP and we will do our best to accommodate you. A variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options will be available for each meal.
How do I get more information? +
If you have any other questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at effieandzade@gmail.com.

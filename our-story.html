Our story

the beginning

How we met

Effie and Zade met in 2019 at MIT and went on the longest date of their lives; what started out as simple plans to visit the Harvard Art Museum turned into an 8-hour day of exploring Boston and Cambridge.

After a dinner at Santouka Ramen, a walk down Mass Ave, a pitstop at Roxy's Arcade Bar (Zade had to show off his pinball skills), and a sunset along the Esplanade, they knew something special was brewing.

A month later, they made it official.

the journey

Long distance

In 2020, the COVID-19 pandemic hit and little did we know we were about to spend the next four years doing long-distance. But, perhaps a blessing in disguise, it gave us valuable freedom during our mid-twenties to go to grad school and start our careers without compromise.

Our Type A personalities prepared a "LDR planning" document to map out our goals, communication expectations, virtual date nights, and visiting schedule. A good excuse to travel, our long-distance led us to California, Utah, Missouri, Colorado, South Korea, Scotland, England, and more.

We strengthened both who were as individuals and as a couple, bringing our best selves to the table.
